Croissant

In Asgard, Odin enjoys a golden croissant with a smile, finding simple pleasure amid cosmic responsibilities.
The flaky layers evoke distant memories, connecting him to both mortals and gods.

Ingredients:

  • Celestial Flour
  • Heavenly Butter
  • Enchanted Yeast
  • Stardust of Salt
  • Nectar of Honeydew Bees
  • Celestial Vanilla Bean
  • Odin's Time Blend (secret blend of spices)

Steps:

  1. In the divine kitchen of Asgard, Odin combines Celestial Flour, Heavenly Butter, and Enchanted Yeast, kneading the dough with mastery and care.
  2. Rolling the dough into a thin sheet, he sprinkles Stardust of Salt to balance the flavors, folding the dough over and over to create countless flaky layers.
  3. Adding a drizzle of Nectar of Honeydew Bees, Odin shapes the dough into crescent forms, giving birth to the iconic croissant shape.
  4. With reverence, he scrapes the seeds of the Celestial Vanilla Bean into the croissants, releasing an enchanting aroma that fills the air.
  5. As the croissants rest and rise, he sprinkles a pinch of Odin's Time Blend, his secret mix of spices, infusing the pastries with his divine wisdom.
  6. Finally, he bakes the croissants to a golden hue, the heavenly scent filling the halls of Asgard, tempting gods and mortals alike.
